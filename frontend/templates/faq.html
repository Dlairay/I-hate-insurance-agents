Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. General Insurance

What types of insurance do you offer? ▼
We offer comprehensive insurance coverage including Health Insurance (Basic and Premium plans), Critical Illness Insurance, Life Insurance (Term and Whole Life), and supplementary coverage such as Disability and Accidental Death benefits.
What documents do I need to upload for a quote? ▼
You can upload existing insurance documents, medical records, or income statements. Our AI system can extract relevant information to provide more accurate quotes. However, you can also complete the process without uploading any documents.
How does your AI agent help me choose the right plan? ▼
Our AI agent analyzes your personal information, lifestyle factors, and coverage needs to provide personalized recommendations. It uses a 3-metric scoring system (affordability, claims ease, coverage ratio) to rank plans based on your specific situation and budget.
Can I buy a policy entirely online? ▼
Yes! Our platform allows you to complete the entire insurance purchase process online. From getting quotes to making payments and receiving your policy documents, everything can be done digitally through our secure platform.
Is my data safe when I use your platform? ▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. All data is stored securely and we never share your information with unauthorized third parties.

2. Pricing & Coverage FAQs

How are my premiums calculated? ▼
Premiums are calculated based on multiple factors including your age, health status, lifestyle habits, coverage amount, and location. Our system also considers smoking habits, exercise frequency, and any high-risk activities you participate in.
Does participating in high-risk activities affect my insurance? ▼
Yes, high-risk activities like skydiving, racing, or extreme sports can affect your premium rates. However, we evaluate each case individually and provide transparent pricing based on your specific risk profile.
What is not covered by my policy? ▼
Exclusions vary by policy type but typically include pre-existing conditions not disclosed during application, self-inflicted injuries, illegal activities, and certain experimental treatments. Full exclusion details are provided in your policy documents.
How can I lower my insurance premium? ▼
You can lower premiums by maintaining a healthy lifestyle, choosing higher deductibles, bundling multiple policies, and avoiding high-risk activities. Regular health check-ups and non-smoking status also help reduce rates.
Can I customise my coverage based on my lifestyle? ▼
Absolutely! Our platform offers flexible coverage options that can be tailored to your specific lifestyle and needs. You can add riders, adjust coverage amounts, and choose deductibles that match your budget and risk tolerance.
